How to cook a steak by Yoon Sung Lee

If you are tired of always eating out and spending money on overpriced steak or just want to learn how to cook a steak this site is just for you.
(Disclaimer: Not written by a professional chef)

Ingredients:

  • A cut of meat (Preferably Ribeye, Sirloin, New York Strip)
  • Cooking oil
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder (optional)
  • Rosemary (optional)
  • Thyme (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Place your pan on the stove and set it to high heat.
  2. Whilst you wait for the pan to get hot, grab a kitchen towel and dab the cut of meat to dry up the surface blood.
  3. Once the pan is hot add your choice of cooking oil and spread it evenly accross the pan and let it rest for a minute to get the oil hot.
  4. Season your meat with salt and pepper on both sides (you could add extra seasoning of your choice such as garlic powder).
  5. Once the pan and oil is hot enough place the meat on one side and gently press on top of it for a couple seconds.
  6. Next step will be personalised to your own liking follow the steps in the given list below to cook your steak how you like it:
    • Rare: 1½ mins per side
    • Medium rare: 2 mins per side
    • Medium: 2¼ mins per side
    • Medium well: 3 mins per side
    • Well-done steak: Cook for about 4-5 mins each side
      • See here the differences between each rarity

  7. Once cooked on both sides of the steak add about 2tbsp of butter on to the pan and let it melt (You could add your own choice of herbs at the same time is desired).
  8. When the butter is melted base it on top of your steak with a spoon for a couple times
  9. Remove the steak from the pan and place it on a plate and let it rest for at least 10 mins
  10. Once the waiting is done cut into the steak and enjoy it!
